Exit Music: Redux Demo

Made by Wretched Team

Genre: Drama, Romance

Status: Demo

Length: Medium

Released on: Tue, 25 August 2020 23:08:37 +0000

Description

The classic story, retold. Exit Music: Redux is a total rewrite and overhaul of the 2018 Radiohead-inspired mod, Exit Music. It has improved on every front, with three distinct acts, all-new spritework & CGs, and a meticulous attention to detail and sound design, while also retaining its iconic Radiohead score (now copyright-friendly!). The demo contains the first act, 'Airbag'.
