Date Club

Made by VenEttore

Genre: Comedy

Status: Discontinued Demo

Length: Very Short

Released on: Sun, 11 Mar 2018 00:00:55 +0000

Description

A mod designed to provide an experience more in line with traditional romance visual novels like Katawa Shoujo. It was planned to have a story branch for each of the four girls that will expand their backstories and personalities, giving you the chance to spend more time with your favorite club member! The path to love and literature won't be an easy one, though.Will you write the way into her heart, or will you break it?
