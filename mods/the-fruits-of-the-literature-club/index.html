UPDATE: This is the full release for Fruits of the Literature Club containing all five chapters for all the routes.Prepare to partake in a thrilling adventure alongside a new main character whose past is shrouded in mystery! This action-packed rollercoaster of a mod is not for the faint of heart. It will keep you on the edge of your seat with it's immersive narration and dialogue.But, after all is said and done The seeds of their future, are sewn in his past.
